About Us

Go to the mall. Go to the park during a concert or festival. Go to the gym like I do, and see t-shirts adorned with athletes, angry, glaring rappers and flash-in-the-pan pop culture horrors. What you won’t see is a single tee championing the countless genius inventors, adventurers, and history-making icons who also happen to be women.

Our tees ‘stem’ from the desire - the need - to fill this yawning chasm. Yeah, that’s right, I said STEM: science, technology, engineering and math. Long, long ago, in a university far away, my engineering professor asked me how I would bring more women into these fields. A silent shrug was my only response…back then.

That’s because stylish tees that send the message that women have been, are, and will always be doing great things hadn’t been printed yet. Images of positive female role models play a role in the STEM solution.

Thanks to SmartyWomyn™, things have changed!
